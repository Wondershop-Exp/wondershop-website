About Sunny

A dynamic and versatile emcee based in Mumbai, Sunny brings over 15 years of hosting experience to weddings, birthdays, baby showers, corporate events, and everything in between — turning every stage into an unforgettable celebration.

The Story

Hosting, for me, is about connecting with people and bringing energy into every room I walk into. Over the years I've anchored weddings, engagements, birthdays, baby showers, corporate events, and cultural programs — and every single one has taught me something new about reading a crowd.

What I love most is turning a stage into an experience, not just a show. Breaking the ice within minutes, keeping every guest involved, and watching a room go from quiet to buzzing with laughter — that's the whole job, and I love every second of it.

Seeing smiles, hearing laughter, and watching families celebrate together is what drives me. Energy on, smiles guaranteed.
