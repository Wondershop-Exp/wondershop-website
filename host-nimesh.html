About Nimesh

A trained actor with 4+ years hosting Mumbai's birthday parties, Nimesh — better known as MC Nimz — brings improv energy, big enthusiasm, and a genuine love for making kids smile to every superhero, sports, and science-themed celebration.

I'm an actor at heart, and that's exactly what I bring to every party — the ability to improvise to any situation and fill the room with high energy and fun vibes.
