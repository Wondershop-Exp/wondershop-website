About Us

Where every birthday becomes a memory worth keeping

Wondershop was born from a simple belief: children deserve celebrations that spark their imagination, not just fill a room. We build end-to-end birthday experiences that are joyful, thoughtful, and completely stress-free for parents.

🗺️
All across Mumbai
From South Mumbai to the suburbs — wherever you celebrate, your child's birthday gets the same care, creativity, and attention to detail.
🌱
Born in 2023
Young, intentional, and built for today's parents. In just over two years, we've grown from a spark of an idea into Mumbai's most-loved children's birthday experience — 200+ events and counting.
🎂
Specialists in Ages 1–12
From a baby's first birthday to a ten-year-old's themed extravaganza — no cookie-cutter packages, just experiences designed to fit your child perfectly.

Shruti and Sidhant didn't set out to start an events company — they set out to solve a problem they felt firsthand as parents. Shruti's years of analytical thinking from BITS Pilani and Sidhant's business instincts sharpened at ISB Hyderabad gave them the tools, but it was their shared love for childhood wonder that gave them the reason. In 2023, they built Wondershop from the ground up in Mumbai — not as vendors, but as fellow parents who know exactly how much a birthday morning matters to a child. Every event they design carries that feeling forward.

Shruti Garg
Co-founder · Experience & Client Journey
Shruti brings warmth, precision, and a genuine love for childhood creativity to everything Wondershop does. A BITS Pilani alumna, she leads experience design and the client journey — making sure every family feels heard, every detail is considered, and every birthday feels like it was made just for their child.
Sidhant Sharma
Co-founder · Operations & Growth
Sidhant is the strategic force and creative energy that keeps Wondershop growing without losing its heart. An ISB Hyderabad alumnus, he oversees operations and partnerships across Mumbai — always asking how to make the next event more magical, more seamless, and more meaningful than the last.
