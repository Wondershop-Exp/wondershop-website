## Design Notes for All Banners
- **Typography:** Headline in Lora (serif, italic for the em-phrase), tag + sub in DM Sans
- **Colour overlay:** Dark gradient from left (for text legibility) fading into the photo
- **CTA button:** Purple (#B59DDE) pill button, white text
- **Consistent badge:** Small "★ 5.0 Google" badge bottom-right on credibility banner
- **Animation:** Subtle fade-in for headline, slight upward slide for sub + CTA
