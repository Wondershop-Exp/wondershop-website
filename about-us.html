Who We Are

Built by parents, for parents

Wondershop was born from a simple belief: children deserve celebrations that spark their imagination, not just fill a room.

🌟 Our Vision

A world where every child grows up knowing their joy matters.

Every birthday should tell a child: you are celebrated, you are seen, and your wonder is the most important thing in the room.

🎯 Our Mission

To design birthday experiences so beautiful that children never forget how it felt.

We curate every detail — decor, story, activities, music, and more — into a seamless, stress-free celebration.

Wondershop Experiences is Mumbai's most-loved children's birthday studio. We don't just organise parties — we design end-to-end experiences that feel personal, joyful, and completely stress-free for parents.

From picking the theme to coordinating decor, activities, host, DJ, pinata, e-invites, photographers, and return gifts — we handle every detail so you can be fully present on your child's special day.

We've built a carefully curated network of hosts, decorators, photographers, and gift suppliers — all vetted for quality and reliability. Every vendor we recommend is someone we'd trust with our own children's birthday.

Meet the Founders

The people behind every celebration

Shruti and Sidhant didn't set out to start an events company — they set out to solve a problem they felt firsthand as parents. Shruti's analytical thinking from BITS Pilani and Sidhant's business instincts from ISB Hyderabad gave them the tools, but it was their shared love for childhood wonder that gave them the reason. In 2023, they built Wondershop from the ground up in Mumbai — not as vendors, but as fellow parents who know exactly how much a birthday morning matters to a child.

Shruti Garg
Co-founder · Experience & Client Journey
🎓 B.E. – BITS Pilani

Shruti graduated from BITS Pilani with a degree in engineering and spent nearly a decade in the corporate world — working across product management and strategy roles at some of India's leading companies. She was good at her work, but always felt most alive when she was designing experiences for the people she loved.

When her own child's birthday came around and she couldn't find a single service that combined quality, creativity, and warmth under one roof, she decided to build it herself. That conviction became Wondershop.

Today, Shruti leads everything that touches the guest experience — from how a theme is visualised to how a parent feels on the morning of the party. Her attention to detail, warmth, and genuine love for children make every Wondershop celebration feel like it was made just for one family.

Sidhant Sharma
Co-founder · Operations & Growth
🎓 MBA – ISB Hyderabad

Sidhant holds an MBA from ISB Hyderabad and built his career in high-growth environments — leading business development and operations at companies that demanded precision, speed, and creative problem-solving. He knew how to scale things. What he was missing was something worth scaling.

Watching Shruti pour herself into building the early version of Wondershop — and seeing how deeply parents responded — was enough. He joined as co-founder and has since been the strategic force that ensures Wondershop grows without losing its soul.

Sidhant oversees vendor relationships, city expansion, and the operational backbone that makes every event run seamlessly. His instinct for building systems with heart is what allows the team to deliver a premium, personalised experience at scale.
